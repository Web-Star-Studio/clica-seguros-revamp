## What Clica Seguros Does

Clica Seguros is one of Brazil's **first fully digital auto insurance brokerages**. The platform allows users to **simulate, compare, and purchase** car insurance policies entirely online, saving up to **60% in minutes**. It connects customers with multiple insurance carriers and shows the best combinations of price, deductible (franquia), and coverage for each profile.

### Core Value Proposition

- **Compare offers from multiple insurers** in seconds
- **Save up to 60%** on auto insurance
- **100% digital experience** — simulation to purchase online
- **Human support via WhatsApp** when needed
- No pushy phone calls, no middlemen

---

## How It Works (3-Step Process)

### 1. Cadastrar (Register)
Provide your personal data and car information. The technology classifies your profile and generates real quotes in seconds.

### 2. Comparar (Compare)
The system analyzes hundreds of proposals in seconds and shows the best combinations of price, deductible, and coverage for your profile.

### 3. Contratar (Purchase)
100% digital experience: online contracting and payment in minutes, with a specialist available on WhatsApp to help you.

## FAQ Topics Covered (Help Page)

The FAQ section educates users about key insurance concepts:

- **Franquia (Deductible)**: How it works, reduced deductible options (50%, 25%)
- **Apolice (Policy)**: What it contains, why it matters
- **Vistoria (Vehicle Inspection)**: When required vs. waived (new cars, renewals)
- **Premio (Premium Pricing)**: Factors that affect pricing — driver profile, vehicle, location, coverage
- **Parcelamento (Installments)**: Credit card up to 12x, debit, boleto
- **Coberturas (Coverage Types)**: Basic (theft, collision, fire) + additional (third-party, rental car, 24h assistance)
- **Carro Reserva (Rental Car)**: How the substitute vehicle service works (7-30+ days)
- **Sinistro (Claims)**: Step-by-step guide on what to do after an accident
